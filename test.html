About Me

Emilia Clarke is a renowned British actress known for her role as Daenerys Targaryen in the hit television series Game of Thrones. With a career spanning film, television, and theater, Emilia has captivated audiences with her versatility and talent. She is also a passionate philanthropist and advocate for various causes.

Education

Emilia Clarke trained at the Drama Centre London, graduating in 2009. Her rigorous training has equipped her with a strong foundation in acting, which she continues to build upon with each new role.
